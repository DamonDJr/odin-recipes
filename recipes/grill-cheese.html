Grilled Cheese

Simply Classic

Ingredients

  • Bread - any kind will do. I prefer sourdough
  • 2 slices of cheese - again any kind. 1 american and 1 sharp cheddar is the ultimate combo
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Melt half tablespoon of butter in large skillet over medium low heat. Place bread in pan and cook until barely crisped.
  2. Remove bread from pan and place cheese slices on the crisp side.The uncooked side should on the outside.
  3. Melt another half tablespoon of butter and place sandwich in pan. Press down on sandwhich. Cook until golden brown. Add more butter, flip, and repeat
