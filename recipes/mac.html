Mac and Cheese

Home

The best mac and cheese you'll ever make. Period.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Rotini Pasta
  • 160g Gruyere
  • 160g Sharp Cheddar
  • 160g Colby Jack
  • 73g Unsalted Butter
  • 50g AP Flour
  • 55g Sour Cream or Creme Faiche
  • 720ml Whole Milk
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • Salt
  • Optional: Mustard Powder, Paprika, Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Season boiling water generously with salt. Add pasta and cook untl just under al dente. Drain pasta and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in same pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 20 seconds. (If using optional spices mix them into flour). Add in flour and mix for about 45 seconds. Slowly pour in milk and while stirring constantly until mixture has thickened
  3. Add cheese, leaving some for later and let them melt into a smooth sauce. Then, stir in creme fraiche or sour cream. Next, add cooked noodles to cheese sauce and stir until completely covered. Lastly, add macaroni and cheese mixture to a cast iron pan, sprinkle remaining cheese over top and place under broiler until cheese has melted and formed a crust.
