You'll need:
  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • MSG
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil
Steps
  1. Mix salt, sugar, and MSG
  2. Cut chicken into bite sized pieces
  3. Sprinkle salt,sugar, MSG mixture over both sides of chicken
  4. Let chicken sit for at least 30 mins or overnight
  5. Mix salt, sugar, MSG, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and powdered sugar into flour
  6. Mix milk into beaten eggs
  7. Preheat oil to 325ºF
  8. Remove chicken from fridge, coat chicken in seasoned flour, then into egg mixture, then back to flour and place on a parchment lined baking sheet. Repeat with remaining chicken
  9. Fry chicken in batches for 4-5 minutes or until internal temp reached 165ºF
