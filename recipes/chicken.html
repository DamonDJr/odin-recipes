Delicious, juicy chicken nuggets

!!!This recipe is done to taste. No measurements!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken Breasts
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • MSG
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 cups of Milk
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast into bite sized pieces. Mix salt, msg, and sugar. Sprinkle mixture over both sides of the chicken. Place chicken in a plastic bag and let sit in fridge for at least 1 hour
  2. Create dredge by adding spices and powdered sugar to flour. Then in a separate bowl add milk to whisked eggs. Coat chicken in flour, then in egg mixture, then back into the flour. Set on a parchment lined baking sheet. Continue until all chicken is coated
  3. Heat oil to 325°F. In batches add chicken to oil and cook for 3-4 minutes. Interal temp should be 165°F.
